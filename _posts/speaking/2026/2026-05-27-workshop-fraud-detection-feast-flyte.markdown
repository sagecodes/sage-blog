A hands-on workshop building an end-to-end fraud detection pipeline with Feast as the feature store and Flyte 2 as the orchestrator. Covers feature store fundamentals, point-in-time correct datasets, orchestrating model training workflows, and deploying a real-time prediction API. Attendees leave with a working model and reusable pipeline code.
