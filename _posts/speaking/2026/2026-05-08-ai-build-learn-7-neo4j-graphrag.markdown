A weekly AI engineering stream where we learn by building. This session covers using Neo4j graph databases for GraphRAG and agent context, exploring the differences between graph and vector databases, knowledge graph applications, and combining graph traversal with semantic search. Includes a hands-on demo, community discussion with practical examples, and an optional community challenge encouraging 30-90 minutes of learning or building during the week.
